About Community SACCO

Learn about our history, mission, and values

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Community SACCO began with a simple mission: to provide accessible financial services to our local community. What started as a small cooperative with just 50 members has grown into a thriving financial institution serving over 5,000 members.

Our Mission

To empower our members through financial education, savings opportunities, and affordable credit facilities that improve their economic well-being.

Our Vision

To be the leading SACCO in the region, recognized for our member-centric approach and commitment to community development.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We conduct all operations with honesty and transparency.
  • Member Focus: Our members' financial success is our primary goal.
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our services to meet evolving needs.
  • Community: We invest in the development of our local community.
