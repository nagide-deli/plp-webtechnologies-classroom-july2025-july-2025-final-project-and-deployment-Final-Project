Our Services

Explore our range of financial products designed for your success

Regular Savings

Build your savings with our flexible regular savings account that earns competitive interest.

  • Minimum deposit: KES 500
  • Interest rate: 7% p.a.
  • No withdrawal fees

Fixed Deposit

Earn higher interest by locking your funds for a specific period.

  • Minimum deposit: KES 10,000
  • Interest rate: Up to 10% p.a.
  • Terms: 3, 6, or 12 months
